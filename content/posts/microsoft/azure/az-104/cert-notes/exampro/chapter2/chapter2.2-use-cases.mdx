## Chapter 2.2 - Use Cases

### Azure AD - Use Case
* Azure Ad can authorize and authenticate to multiple sources:
	* To your on-premise AD
	* To your web-application
	* Allow users to login with their IpD (Identity Provider) eg. Facebook or Google
	* To Office 365 or Azure Microsoft
