# MS-102 Certification Notes
## Day 6.8 - Privileged Identity Management

### Privileged Identity Management
* Framework for managing privileged identities to provide Just-in-time Access to administrative actions
* Can assign time-bound administrative access
* Enforce multi-factor authentication for administrative actions
* Manage requests to include justification
* Set up notifications
* Conduct Access Reviews to reduce unnecessary Administrative Rights
* Prevents accidental removal of Global Administrator and Privileged Role Administrator Role Assignments
