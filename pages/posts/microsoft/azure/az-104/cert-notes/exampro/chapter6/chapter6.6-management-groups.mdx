# AZ-104 Certification Notes
## Chapter 6.6 - Management Groups

### Azure Management Groups
Managing multiple subscriptions (accounts) into a hierarchal structure.
* Each directory is given a single top-level management group called the "Root" management group
* All subscriptions within a management group automatically inherit the conditions applied to the management group

### Practice Quiz
* All subscriptions within a management group automatically inherit the conditions applied to the management group.
	* **True**
	* False

* What are Azure Management Groups?
	* Management groups are containers that help you manage access, policy, and compliance across multiple subscriptions.
